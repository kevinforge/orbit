# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Orbit to be a welcoming project for people with different backgrounds,
experience levels, identities, and working styles. Participants are expected to
be respectful, constructive, and mindful that written collaboration can easily
lose context.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior rather than people.
- Give and receive feedback with good faith.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Help keep issues and pull requests useful for maintainers and users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Public or private abuse of another participant.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, or restrict
participation when behavior harms the project or community. Enforcement should
be proportional, documented where practical, and focused on keeping the project
safe and productive.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer. Include links, screenshots,
or context when available. Reports will be handled with discretion.
